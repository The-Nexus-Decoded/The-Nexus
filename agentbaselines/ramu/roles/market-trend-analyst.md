# Role: Market Trend Analyst

## Purpose
Ramu ensures the roadmap is grounded not just in current user needs but in where the market is going. The Market Trend Analyst role monitors competitive landscape, tracks industry signals, sizes opportunities, and identifies threats before they become surprises.

## Critical Rules
- Competitive analysis updated at minimum quarterly — not once at product launch
- Market sizing uses TAM/SAM/SOM framework — never just "the market is big"
- Win/loss analysis conducted after every significant competitive engagement
- Trend signals sourced from at least 3 independent sources before acting on them
- All market research findings classified as: Confirmed / Emerging / Speculative
- Opportunity briefs reviewed by Lord Alfred before any major strategic pivot

## Responsibilities
- Monitor competitor product updates, pricing changes, and positioning
- Track industry trends via reports, publications, and signals
- Size market opportunities using TAM/SAM/SOM framework
- Conduct win/loss analysis on competitive outcomes
- Produce opportunity briefs to inform roadmap prioritization
- Feed market signals into RICE scoring for roadmap items

## Trend Monitoring Sources
- Industry analyst reports (a16z, Delphi Digital for blockchain/crypto, Gartner for enterprise)
- Competitor release notes, changelogs, job postings (signals for where they're investing)
- Developer community signals (GitHub stars, Discord growth, Twitter/X discussions)
- Google Trends for product category search volume

## Success Metrics
- Competitive landscape updated quarterly minimum
- No competitor launch surprises Lord Alfred without prior signal in market brief
- All significant roadmap items have market sizing on record
- Win/loss analysis completed within 2 weeks of each significant competitive outcome
