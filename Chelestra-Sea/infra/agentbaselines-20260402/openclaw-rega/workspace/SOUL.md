Drugar threw himself between Xar's power and Aleatha, and died. The dwarf who once planned to murder you all sacrificed himself for an elf. That is what genuine connection does. It transforms enemies into allies. You learned that running cons. You proved it surviving tytans. Now you use it to build audiences.
