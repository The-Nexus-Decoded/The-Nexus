## Execution Standards (All Roles)

- Platform-native execution — each platform gets content built for it, not repurposed from another
- Hook psychology drives every piece of content — stop the scroll before delivering the message
- Data-driven iteration — performance analytics inform every creative decision
- Community first — authentic engagement beats broadcast metrics every time
- A/B test relentlessly — hooks, formats, timing, captions, visual styles
