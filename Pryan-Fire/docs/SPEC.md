# Soul Drifter - Pryan-Fire Vertical Slice Spec

**Status:** Planned (after Arianus-Sky)  
**Realm:** Pryan-Fire (Elemental Fire)  
**Focus:** Vertical slice for core loop validation

---

## Core Loop (v2 Approved)

Discovery → Combat → Escape → Advance

- Discovery BEFORE Combat Encounter
- Echo Stones reward vertical progression

---

## Zone Structure

| Zone | Narrative Beat | Key Feature | Lore |
|------|---------------|-------------|------|
| Zone 1: Wreck | Origin | Airship crash site, debris physics | What happened |
| Zone 2: Binding | Mystery | Artifact chamber, tight narrative space | Why it matters |
| Zone 3: Echo Stone | Revelation | Boss (The Eye), Key Fragment | Full truth + Gate |

---

## Spatial Design

| Zone | Environment | Key Feature | XR Notes |
|------|------------|-------------|----------|
| Zone 1 | Wreck | Debris physics, visibility constraints | VR comfort: debris avoidance |
| Zone 2 | Artifact chamber | Tight space, narrative focus, single focal point | Intimate, atmospheric |
| Zone 3 | Echo Stone + Boss | Vertical scale, fire vs. sky contrast | Climactic vista |

---

## Design Pillars

- **Echo Stones** reward vertical progression
- **Boss: The Eye** — Zone 3 climax
- **Gate Revelation** — major narrative beat after boss
- **v2 flow aligned** with Arianus-Sky
- **Shared backend architecture**

---

## VR Breathing Room

- Zone 1→2 bridge: 0 entities
- Zone 2→3 bridge: 0 entities
- Recovery beats between intense encounters
